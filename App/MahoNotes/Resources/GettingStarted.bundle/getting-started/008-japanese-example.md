# 8. 日本語メモの例

This note demonstrates Maho Notes' Japanese language features. Try searching for any of the Japanese text below!

## 基本の挨拶 — Basic Greetings

| 日本語 | 読み方 | English |
|--------|--------|---------|
| {おはようございます|o-ha-yō go-za-i-ma-su} | ohayō gozaimasu | Good morning |
| {こんにちは|kon-ni-chi-wa} | konnichiwa | Hello |
| {こんばんは|kon-ban-wa} | konbanwa | Good evening |
| {ありがとうございます|a-ri-ga-tō go-za-i-ma-su} | arigatō gozaimasu | Thank you |

## 助詞の使い方 — Particles

Japanese particles are small words that mark grammatical relationships:

- **は** (wa) — Topic marker: {私|わたし}**は**{学生|がくせい}です。(*I am a student.*)
- **が** (ga) — Subject marker: {猫|ねこ}**が**いる。(*There is a cat.*)
- **を** (wo) — Object marker: {本|ほん}**を**{読|よ}む。(*Read a book.*)
- **に** (ni) — Direction/time: {学校|がっこう}**に**{行|い}く。(*Go to school.*)

## 例文 — Example Sentences

> {今日|きょう}は{天気|てんき}がいいですね。
> *The weather is nice today, isn't it?*

> {宇宙|うちゅう}の{研究|けんきゅう}は{面白|おもしろ}い。
> *Space research is interesting.*
